MENTAL HEALTH AND A HAPPIER WORLD

Few weeks ago “world mental health day” was celebrated and I have just one question to ask you.

How are you?

I would like you to deeply reflect on that question before you answer because I know your default response would be “I am fine” but are you truly fine?

Sometime back I watched a video on Tiktok in which 10 women were asked if they were okay and their responses were all an affirmative “yes”. They were asked that same question again and upon proper reflection, turns out that they all had problems they were dealing with.

According to Martin Luther King Jr “Nothing in the world is more dangerous than sincere ignorance”.
